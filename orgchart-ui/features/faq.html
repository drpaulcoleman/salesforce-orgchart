FAQ

My personal information is not being displayed correctly, how can it be fixed?

OrgChart pulls information directly from each employee’s Salesforce user profile and User.ManagerId. Please check the information on your profile and ensure your manager is filled in. If you have changed your name or need additional help correcting your information, see your team’s Help Article (configure the link in features/faq.html when you deploy). Updates may take up to 24 hours to appear in OrgChart.

Where does the chart data come from?

Active Salesforce users and reporting hierarchy, subject to your org’s sharing and field-level security.

Need more help?

Open your team’s Help Article or internal documentation for org-chart policies and data quality guidelines.
